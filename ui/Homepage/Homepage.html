What is SwapIt All about?

SwapIt is the Tinder of swaps! We all remember how boring and useless dating sites were before the revolutionary impact of Tinder's machine learning matching algorithm, right? Well, imagine sitting at home, seeing your old PlayStation game, phone, or bag. Taking a picture of it and swiping left or right to find a suitable swap.
